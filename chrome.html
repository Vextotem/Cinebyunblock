If your internet provider blocks access to certain domains, following these steps you can avoid any blockage
As already published on sites such as trending pie, Xataka, Genbeta, El Androide Feliz o Menéame now you can activateECH to avoid blocking by your operator and make your browsing more secure and private.
 
If you want to know a little more about how it works here
 
To do this you must follow the following steps (You must have your browser updated to at least version 117.)
It works for both PC and mobile.
Type the term in the address bar "chrome://flags". This will take you to the configuration screen, where you'll see the experimental features.
Once there, you have to use the search engine to find and activate these functions.
  • encrypted-client-hello
  • use-dns-https-svcb-alpn
 
Now, go to Chrome settings, click on Privacy and security, and within this section go to the section of security.
 
In here, activate the option Use secure DNS and choose the option of Cloudflare or Google.
 
Once this is done, all that remains is to restart the browser and Done ! You can now browse without restrictions!
